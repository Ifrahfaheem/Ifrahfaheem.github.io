Description

Kylian Mbappé, born on December 20, 1998, is a French professional footballer widely regarded as one of the brightest talents in the sport. He gained international recognition for his incredible speed, skill, and versatility on the field. Mbappé began his career at AS Monaco, making his senior debut at just 16. His breakout season came in 2016-2017, where he helped Monaco win Ligue 1 and reached the UEFA Champions League semifinals. In 2017, he transferred to Paris Saint-Germain (PSG) on loan, a move later made permanent. With PSG, he continued to flourish, winning numerous domestic titles. He also played a pivotal role in France's 2018 FIFA World Cup victory, becoming the second teenager in history to score in a World Cup final.
