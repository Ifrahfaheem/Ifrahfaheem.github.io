Description

Manchester City FC, based in Manchester, England, is a prominent football club founded in 1880. They play their home games at the Etihad Stadium with a capacity of around 53,000. Owned by the City Football Group, the team has achieved notable success under manager Pep Guardiola, securing multiple Premier League titles, FA Cups, and League Cups. The club's nickname is "City" or "The Citizens," and their colors are sky blue. As of September 2021, Pep Guardiola was the manager.
